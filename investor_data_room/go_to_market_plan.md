# Optified Platform: Go-To-Market Plan
*High-Affluence Channels & Clinical Integrations*

---

## 1. Target Audience Segmentation
1. **Primary Segment (Longevity Centers):** Premium private health clinics specializing in preventative, longevity, and anti-aging treatments (average client spend: $10k+/year).
2. **Secondary Segment (Performance Coaches):** Sports nutritionists, functional coaches, and elite athletic trainers.

---

## 2. Channel Strategy
* **Direct B2B Sales:** Direct sales outreach targeting clinical directors at leading longevity hubs in San Francisco, Zurich, Munich, and Tokyo.
* **Laboratory Integrations (Trojan Horse):** Partnering with test providers (e.g. Genova Diagnostics, Microbiomix, LabCorp) to bundle Optified dashboard licenses with test kit orders.

---

## 3. Positioning and Pricing
* **Positioning:** "The Operating System for Longevity Biology."
* **Enterprise Tier:** $499/month for clinics with up to 5 practitioners, including custom lab branding.

## Slide 2: The Longevity Paradox
* **The Problem:** 
  - Healthcare is reactive, waiting for disease. Longevity medicine is proactive, but highly complex.
  - Clinicians are overwhelmed by disparate multi-omic data (Genetics, Gut Microbiome, Cardiorespiratory VO2 Max, Daily Wearables).
  - Processing one patient requires hours of manual lab report parsing and study correlation.

## Slide 3: The Optified Solution
* **Our Platform:**
  - A secure, HIPAA-compliant Go + HTMX + Python system.
  - Automated PDF report parsers translating clinical PDFs into SQL schemas.
  - **KnowsItAll AI-Agent:** RAG assistant grounded in leading global journals (NEJM, Lancet, Nature Medicine).
  - **Clinical Cockpit:** Visual client logs, customizable biomarker targets, automated anomaly trackers.

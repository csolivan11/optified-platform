# Optified Platform: Revenue Growth Plan
*Subscription Optimization & Margins Projections*

---

## 1. SaaS Tiers structure
* **Base Seats License:** $199/month per clinician for core queue monitors, RAG chat assistant, and notes editor.
* **Client Profile Add-on:** $19/month per active client profile. A clinic with 100 active clients yields $2,099/month.

---

## 2. Laboratory Affiliate Commissions
* **B2B Affiliate Margin:** Optified charges a 15% handling fee on labs processed through the platform (Microbiomix, Genova, LabCorp, Quest). 

---

## 3. Supplement Dropship Integration
* Custom supplement protocols link directly to partner API pharmacies (e.g. Fullscript), generating a 20% commission on automated monthly orders.

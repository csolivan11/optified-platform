# Optified Platform: User Adoption & Engagement Plan
*Retention Loops and Behavioral Compliance*

---

## 1. Seamless Onboarding Flow
* **Frictionless Wearable Connection:** Direct OAuth authorization widgets for Oura, Whoop, and Apple Health.
* **Intake Questionnaires:** Collect baseline cognitive load metrics and diet restrictions during sign-up to pre-populate custom biomarkers limits.

---

## 2. Product-Led Growth (PLG) Loops
* **Secure PDF sharing:** PDF reports exportable in one-click with full AMA bibliography formatting, driving clinical referral loops.
* **Interactive SVG Graph Insights:** Gamified explorer mapping encourages users to investigate supplement interactions.

---

## 3. Compliance Boosters
* **Supplement Calendars:** Visual 30-day dot calendars providing rewards feedback on protocol compliance.
* **Telehealth consultation triggers:** Prompt users to schedule reviews whenever biomarker deviation alerts trigger.
